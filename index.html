Instructions:

At the start of the game you will be given a random number that is the number to get to.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

You win the game by matching your score to the nummber to get. You lose the game if your score goes above the number to get.

Each time you win or lose, the game restarts and the crystal values will change also.
